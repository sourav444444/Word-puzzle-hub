Why is Letter Boxed so popular among all NYT games in 2025

The New York Times provides captivating puzzles to its loyal users. If you open the New York Times app, you will find a diverse set of puzzles. You can access 10 different puzzles in one app. These puzzles are the Mini, crossword, spelling bee, Wordle, strands, connections, letter boxed, tiles, and Sudoku.

Overview of the top 7 NYT games

The crossword: The crossword is a complex game that demands your analytical and critical thinking. This is a clue-based game that provides clues, and you have to answer them correctly.

Spelling Bee: The gameplay of Spelling Bee is unique. There are seven distinct letters arranged in a honeycomb-shaped grid. Puzzlers must use all the letters to create words. It is more of a strategy-based game.

Letter Boxed: Another interesting game in our list is Letter Boxed. If you have great vocabulary knowledge, you can easily win this game. The structure of the game is simple. The game has a square-shaped box and 12 distinct letters arranged around it. You can solve the game by creating two or three words. You can visit the link to learn tips and tricks to solve the puzzle like a pro.

Wordle: This word game comes under the New York Times puzzle section on a daily basis. In this game, players will be given six guesses to create words. The words must contain five letters. The simplicity of the game has attracted many puzzle enthusiasts worldwide. At the beginning, the game may look difficult, but once you have enough practice, this game will be a cakewalk.

Tiles: The gameplay of tiles is slightly different from traditional word games. In this game, you need to focus more on patterns and colours. The grid is made up of various colours and shapes. You need to match those tiles according to the pattern or colour.

Strands: The New York Times has recently published the game. It is in beta version, which means it is being tested. The game contains a grid and a theme hidden in it. You need to form words related to the theme.

Sudoku: Sudoku is a little bit tough for beginners. However, it has a huge base of loyal players. It is a number game. You need to recognize numbers to create a proper pattern.

Why is Letter Boxed so popular in 2025

Despite many word games available under the puzzle section, the popularity of Letter Boxed has been growing. As the game provides different challenges every day, many students prefer this game for educational purposes. It helps both vocabulary and spelling skills.
